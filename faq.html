Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to fix an iPad screen?

Every model iPad has a different repair technique so times vary between models. Most iPad models take anywhere from 1-2 hours depending on the extent of the damage to the screen. Internal components can take a little longer as we have to remove the screen without damage. Please keep in mind, this does not include transit time (to and from your office).

The corners of my iPad are dented from the fall.
Can you fix this?

Unless we were to replace the entire housing, there is no way to make it perfect. However, we do have tools and machines which help reshape the corners to the best it can be. We also test every screen to ensure it can withstand pressure in that spot before returning your device (so that there are no magically appearing cracks when we give it back).

I cracked the screen on my iPad and the digitizer (touch screen) stopped working. Is this fixable?

99% of problems that occur with your iPad are fixable. Even if it does not turn on, we can usually get the device to work again. Digitizer replacements are done all the time.

My iPad Screen isn't showing an image anymore.
Do you still fix that?

Yes! This would be considered the LCD. We fix them all the time!

What types of replacement screens do you use?

Depending on the model of the device, the replacement parts we use vary. Some models (the older ones) only have aftermarket parts available. Out of the 3 tiers of aftermarket, we use the highest grade to prevent defects which are more prominent in the lower quality parts. We also test our parts to the same standards as the factories. For the newer iPads, we only use OEM quality displays. IPad's are known for their beautiful and responsive displays. It would not make sense if we fixed them with aftermarket parts. Plus, they just look horrible!

Can you pick up, fix, and return my iPad in the same day?

This depends on a few things:
1- Distance from our location
2- Extent of damage to the iPad
3- Type of repair
4- Time Schedule

As long as the iPad is picked up early enough, we can generally complete the entire repair and drop off before the end of day. Just let us know your schedule and we will do our best to work around it.

What models of the iPad do you fix?

We fix all of them! As long as the parts are available, we have the tools and expertise to fill all models including the iPad Mini 4, iPad 6th Generation, iPad Pro 12.9 Inch, and even the new ones with face ID. Check out our pricing!
