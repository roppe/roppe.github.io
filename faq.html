FAQ: Tile and Tub Reglazing Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is bathroom reglazing?

A: Bathroom refinishing is basically the process of applying a durable coating to make the fixtures in your bathroom look like new. It can cost just 10% of the cost of remodeling job.

Q: Why should I choose you and not another company?

A: Remember, you get what you pay for. Even though you may find cheaper companies in Houston, TX, we assure you that you won't get the same results with them. We use only the best materials, solutions and tools to make sure we do an excellent job every time. Our experts have more than 20 years of combined experience.

Q: How long does it take your experts to finish the reglazing process?

A: : Generally, it takes our technicians anywhere between 2 and 4 hours to complete the resurfacing project. In case the tub has been previously reglazed, it will take an extra hour for our expert to strip the old glaze.

Q: Can I use my tub, sink or toilet after the reglazing process is complete?

A: Unfortunately, no. The glaze needs to cure, and this takes around 24 hours. We advise against using the surface during this time.

Q: How long does the glaze last?

A: If you properly maintain the surface, the glaze should last 10 to 15 years.

Q: What if I have a problem with my refinished surface after the job is complete? What is your warranty policy?

A: Residential and commercial clients receive a warranty, of course .Read the  Warranty Agreement for more information.

Q: Can you tell that the tub has been resurfaced?

A: : If the reglazing process has been done by an expert, it's almost impossible to spot a reglazed tub. Even though a professional bathtub refinisher can tell the difference, most individuals can't.

Q:Is the refinished tub or shower slippery?

A: Yes, the refinished surface is just like the old surface. It is slippery when wet. However, you can simply order our Anti-Slip treatment and we'll apply a comfortable, adherent coating to the bathtub or floor. Slipping is almost impossible on this kind of surface.

Q: Can you replace the ugly caulking around my tub?

A: Of course, we will replace the caulking around the edges of your bathtub. If you think you need our expert to do this, please notify us before your appointment.

Q: Can you change the color of my tub?

A: Yes, we can change the color of tubs, countertops and tiles. Check out the available colors here

Q:What can you reglaze?

A: Sparkle Bathtub Refinishing Houston can reglaze almost any surface you can think of. From counters, countertops and tiles to sinks, shower stalls and floors, we can refinish everything. We are also experts at regrouting tiles and recaulking bathtubs.

Q: Why would I resurface the tiles and the floor?

A: If you want to change the color of the tiles and floor, the most affordable option is to order a resurfacing service. Instead of replacing the floor and wall tiles, we can reglaze them in no time. Yes, we can even change the color of your tiles and fixtures. Our technicians can help you with regrouting services as well. Take a look at our Tile Refinishing Page for more information.

Q: What is countertop refinishing?

A: This is basically the same process we use to bring tubs and tiles back to life. We can easily change the look and feel of your kitchen and bathroom. Our experts can even give your countertops the look and feel of granite. The whole process usually takes less than one day.

Q: How should I care for the reglazed surface?

A: It's not difficult to maintain the reglazed surface. Read the information on the Warranty Page for detailed instructions.

Q: Does the refinishing process smell?

A: All the solutions we use are non-toxic and environmentally friendly. In addition, our experts ventilate the area to make sure the smell is dispersed as efficiently as possible. You shouldn't be able to smell anything 3 hours after the reglazing process is complete.

Q: How can I help your experts with the reglazing process?

A: : The area we will work in needs to be dry and clear of all items. Also, make sure the faucet is not dripping. Turning it off or covering it is not a solution, so you should hire a licensed plumber to repair the faucet. We will cover the entire area around the tub ourselves to make sure the materials don't touch any other surface.

Q:I'm planning to renovate. Should I reglaze the tub?

A: If you are planning to renovate, we advise you to reglaze the tub or tiles after the renovations are done. If the reglazed items are damaged during the renovation, you are not covered by our warranty.

Q: I have a plumbing problem. Can you help?

A: Unfortunately, our experts are not trained to do plumbing work. Hire a licensed plumber if you need help with the plumbing. If it's only a faucet cover or drain cover that needs to be changed, we can help you though
