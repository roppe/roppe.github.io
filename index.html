Frequently Asked Questions

YOU GOT QUESTIONS? WE GOT ANSWERS!

A Refund Settlement Product is made available to taxpayers at the completion of their tax return when a refund is due. The refund settlement product is for taxpayers who file their taxes electronically but do not have the out of pocket funds to pay for their tax preparation fees upfront. Once the IRS or state taxing authority deposits the taxpayer's refund proceeds into a designated account set up through TA Financial authorized bank partner, the taxpayer will receive their tax refund by a disbursement option offer by TA Financial.
TA Financial enrollment is open all year; however, there maybe limitations to services rendered during certain enrollment periods.
Tax Professionals A Happy Cash Advance for tax professionals allows a tax professional to have cash flow before and during the tax season to assist with payroll, office setup, marketing, and other related expenses. Fees may apply. Taxpayers A Happy Cash Advance is a cash advance loan based upon your client’s refund and is not the refund itself. The Happy Cash Advance is repaid when the client’s tax refund has been funded by the IRS or State Agency and separate fees apply to the taxpayer and or the tax professional.
Tax preparation fees are released once the client’s tax refund has been funded. Depending on your financial institution it can take up 2 to 3 business days for funds to be deposited to your account.
Depending on the type of tax return that is being filed, a refund is normally issued within 7 to 14 business days from the IRS or State Agency and TA Financial process the refund immediately to the disbursement option that was selected. The IRS or State Agency dictates the release of the refund.
