Care and Maintenance

After Reglazing

Drying times is 24-36 hrs on most surfaces. Please do not use bathroom / tub for that time

After Reglazing Cleaning Instructions

  • Do not use for 24 hours, do not clean for 1 week after refinishing
  • No abrasive cleaners (Comet, Ajax, Bleach, S.O.S, soft scrub, chlorine).
  • Only use products that are safe on refinished or reglazed surfaces
  • Clean at least weekly

Recommended Product

  • Lysol 4 in 1 All Purpose (available in any grocery store)
  • If any build up occurs use regular 409 with cleaner
  • Clean at least weekly
  • Clean at least weekly

Cleaning Tools

  • Soft cloth- can use a microfiber cloth used to wash cars or a towel
  • Soft Sponge

If you have build up that does not come off with the above-mentioned items, you can use Tuffy. Please be careful with the use of this product.

Bath Mats

  • Please allow 1 full week for the refinishing materials to completely cure before using any bath mats.
  • Do not use bath mats with suction cups as this can cause the paint to peel.
